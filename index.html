Our Services

What We Offer

Dry Cleaning

Dry cleaning uses a chemical solvent, usually perchloroethylene, for the removal of stains. As the name entails, dry cleaning does not wet the item; the method keeps the item dry while treating the stain and cleaning the piece entirely. It's important to know what fabric needs dry cleaning. Such fabric (such as your suits) should never be laundry washed.

Laundry Washing

Laundry washing uses water to act as a solvent for soaps and detergents. Bleach and fabric softeners are also used to treat stains and the material itself. Laundry washing is said to be safer than dry cleaning, since no strong chemicals are used, but the most important thing is know which method to apply in the right situation.

Stain Removal

There different stains common to clothes that could sometimes be very difficult to get rid of. This stains includes: Fruit Stain, Coffee Stains, Mud Stains, Blood Stains e.t.c. Each of these stains require special methods and solvent to remove them. Our personals are trained to help you get rid of such stains in a matter of hours.

Ironing

Each garment is ironed and hung on hangers and are packaged in protective plastic covers to keep them clean and wrinkle-free when delivered back to you.

Shoe Washing

People spend hours scrubbing and washing their shoes and end up not getting the desired results, this can be fasting. It's just a matter of applying the right techniques and right use of chemicals and polish and your shoes will look as good as new.

Rug Washing

It's important to give your carpets and rugs a good laundry wash at least once every 4 months. We do this for you at the capital laundry for a very affordable price.
